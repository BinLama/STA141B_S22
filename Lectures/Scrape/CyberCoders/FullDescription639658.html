Job Title: Data Scientist
Job Location: 3x per week onsite in Financial District, NY
Salary: $110-130k
Requirements: 4+ years experience Data Analytics, Python and/or R. SQL, Machine Learning

Working for us means working with a close-knit team of diverse and gifted individuals who are dedicated to furthering social justice by delivering a life-altering education for kids.

Our nonprofit education management company is seeking a Data Scientist to join our team! Our company reshapes what is possible in public education, and we are known for delivering education to students from all backgrounds. As one of the fastest growing and largest charter school networks, we pride ourselves on excellence and outperforming other schools in every district of NY.

This position will be onsite 3x per week in the Financial District, and 2x per week remote.

What You Will Be Doing

Collect, maintain, and analyze all data about our recruitment, enrollment, and retention pipeline
Analyze family life cycle and lifetime value to make recommendations
Develop and conduct trainings for colleagues to enable self-service for recurring analytics tasks

What You Need for this Position

Bachelors degree
4+ years of experience in data analysis in a customer relationship management, marketing, or consumer research environment
Python and/or R
Writing and understanding SQL queries
Highly functional knowledge of CRM software


Plus skills:
Ability to develop scripts to enable Natural Language Processing (NLP) solutions to support consumer analysis
So, if you are a Data & Analytics Program Manager / Data Scientist with experience, please apply today!

Applicants must be authorized to work in the U.S.

Additional ways to apply

Preferred Skills

  • Data Analytics
  • CRM Software
  • Python
  • R
  • SQL
